Women Cabinet Ministers

Women hold just 8% of cabinet positions in Nigeria, well below the global average and the African continental average of 24%.

Source: UN Women 2025

Women State Governors

Nigeria has never elected a female state governor. All 36 governorship seats are held by men, making it one of the starkest gender gaps in Nigerian politics.

Source: INEC 2025
