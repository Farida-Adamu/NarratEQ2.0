About the Scorecard

The NarratEQ Gender Economy Scorecard is an annual assessment that grades Nigeria across six domains of gender equality. Each domain is scored 0–100 based on publicly available data from credible sources.

Grading Scale

A 80–100
B 60–79
C 40–59
D 20–39
F 0–19

Data Sources

  • WEF Gender Gap Report 2025
  • INEC Election Data 2023
  • WILAN Leadership Report 2025
  • BudgIT Nigeria 2024
  • NBS Labour Force Survey 2024
  • UN Women Data Hub
  • World Bank Gender Portal
  • Reykjavik Index 2024

Methodology

Each domain comprises 3–5 indicators drawn from the most recent publicly available data. Indicators are normalised to a 0–100 scale and weighted equally within each domain. The composite score is the unweighted average of all six domain scores.
